"HOME TRACK / NEW TRACK FOR GMG RACING'S JAMES SOFRONAS AND ALEX WELCH AS THEY HEAD TO LIME ROCK PARK FOR THE NEXT ALMS GTC RACE"

GMG's next race in the ALMS series takes place at a familiar track for GMG Principle James Sofronas, but a new one for first time teammate Alex Welch. James is looking forward to returning to a home track, Lime Rock. His first race laps took place there 17 years ago. He considers it a homecoming, as he grew up 70 miles away in Pound Ridge, NY. "I love this track" said James. "This is where I got my first wheel to wheel racing with SCCA, and I love coming back here. So many great memories with friends and family coming to the track, as well as some great races including my first professional podium many years ago. The uphill and downhill sections are so much fun, and I can't wait to get behind the wheel of the GMG Porsche."
Joining James this time around will be new GTC driver Alex Welch. This will be his first ALMS and visit to Lime Rock Park.
Alex added, "I am very excited to be running my first ALMS race at Lime Rock with James Sofronas and GMG. James and his team have done an outstanding job preparing me for this big step. James is my personal driving coach and has been at my side during every 'IMSA GT3 Cup Challenge by Yokohama' race this season. His experience, enthusiasm and motivation have helped me to better myself both on and off the track. The GMG team is very professional and has made it possible for me to make every session and finish every race thus far this season."

This year's American Le Mans Northeast Grand Prix is scheduled for 2:05 p.m. ET on Saturday, July 9. Live video coverage is available on ESPN3.com in the US and americanlemans.com for international users. Worldwide radio coverage is available on American Le Mans Radio at americanlemans.com.
