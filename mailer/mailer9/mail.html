GMG ROARS INTO LONG BEACH WITH 5 WORLD CHALLENGE CARS AND 1 ALMS CAR!

Come and enjoy an action packed weekend as GMG will be competing in both the Pirelli World Challenge Series and ALMS.

Drivers James Sofronas, Dino Crescentini, Tomy Drissi, Bill Ziegler and Brent Holden will be carrying the flag for GMG in the WC race while Bret Curtis will have James doing double duty driving in the ALMS Series.

GMG looks to repeat its 2009 overall WIN and 2010 podium in World Challenge GT, while in ALMS, James Sofronas and Bret Curtis try to improve on their 2nd place finish in 2010.

GMG will also be hosting a large display adjacent to the World Challenge Paddock showcasing GMG tuned road cars in conjunction with Stoptech Brakes. Please stop by and say hi and help cheer us on this weekend!

GMG's schedule for the weekend is as follows:
Saturday:
ALMS Race: 4:30 - 6:30

Sunday:
World Challenge Race: 4:15
