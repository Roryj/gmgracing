GMG friends and customers, please come join us for a day of fun in Los Angeles at Round 3 of the Targa Trophy on Saturday August 27, 2011. This is an excellent opportunity to involve friends, family, wives and girlfriends to take part in a fun event that does not require a helmet or race harness. Enjoy some of the best roads that Southern California has to offer followed by a great after party, all set in a Monte Carlo Rallye style start... Remember, this is not a race, but an event that does require a co-driver and some navigational skills... most of all it's a great event to enjoy with fellow car buffs. Ferrari is the featured marque so we would love to see some of our current Ferrari customers join us for the event with other fellow GMG customers. When registering please be sure to mention that you are a GMG customer to ensure you get the VIP treatment and avoid the selection process hassles.

  

Targa Trophy presents Event #3 in our series of Experience Events and this time around we are holding court in one of So Cal's most car crazy regions - Los Angeles.

The Targa Trophy Experience special event series is designed to give new and current patrons just a taste of what Targa Trophy events are all about and gear people up for our blow out TRIPLE CROWN main event this fall! For our third event of the year, we are creating a unique rally format that will traverse some of the best and most scenic roads/locations Los Angeles has to offer. This LA event will also tie in some very interesting checkpoints at various areas in LA that will add to the excitement and fun factor. Playing off our last events success, we are looking forward to a large group of returning Targa Trophy members and a new crop of entrants looking to get their first taste of the Targa Trophy Road Rally event experience.

For more info and to confirm your entry into Targa Trophy Experience Event #3 Los Angeles - go to www.targatrophy.com
