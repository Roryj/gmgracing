World Challenge Championships on VERSUS!

Don't forget to tune-in to VERSUS this Saturday, August 28th to watch James Sofronas, Dino Crescentini, Bill Ziegler and the GMG race team as they challenge all corners at Mid-Ohio. The World Challenge series competed at the Mid-Ohio Sports Car Course for Round Eight and Nine of this season's series. The course is a 13-Turn, 2.258 mile road course that is famous for it's sports car racing history.
