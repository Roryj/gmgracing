GMG Track Day at WILLOW SPRINGS "BIG TRACK" Dec 2nd, 2010

Global Motorsports Group would like to invite you to our Exclusive GMG Track Day at Willow Springs Raceway.

The event will take place on Thursday, December 2nd from 8am to 5pm.

REQUIREMENTS // DETAILS:

This is an open track "lapping day" for both Street and Race cars and an opportunity to drive your car while in a safe and controlled environment.

The GMG Race Team will be on hand to assist with any car or technical questions.
GMG can assist with car set up advice:

  • Tire pressures, sway bar settings, shock settings, etc to help you dial in your car.
  • Driver Coaching is also available at an additional cost
  • Track tires can be purchased, both new and used, at GMG and we can mount and balance them as well as set up your car with a track alignment.

Additional options include: in-car video system and data acquisition for driver coaching and lap-time review

Helmets are required

Corner workers will be provided

Schedule:

  • 8:30 - 8:45am - Mandatory participant driver meeting
  • 8:45 - 9am - Pace laps - Get familiar with the track
  • 9:00am - 12 noon Alternate 30 min sessions with Street cars vs Race cars
  • 12:00 - 1:00pm - Lunch
  • 1:00 - 4:30pm - Open Track for both Street and Race cars

Additional rental options include: in-car video system and data acquisition for driver and lap-time review (Please inquire for pricing)

Price for the entire day including lunch is $595.00

Please contact GMG Racing for sign up at 714.432.1582, or feel free to email us at sales@gmgracing.com

Space is limited for maximum track time, so register early!
