WILLOW SPRINGS "BIG TRACK" TRACK DAY

Global Motorsports Group would like to invite you to a track day at willow springs raceway, dubbed "The Fastest Road In The West".

The event will take place on Thursday, February 11 From 8am to 5pm.

REQUIREMENTS // DETAILS:

This is an open track "lapping day" and an opportunity to drive your car on the fastest road in the west, The Willow Springs "Big Track"

This Event is open to all stock street vehicles and track specific cars.

Helmets are required

Corner workers / Safety workers will be at the track for the entire day.

Schedule:

  • 8:30 - 8:45am - Mandatory participant driver meeting
  • 8:45 - 9am - Pace laps - Get familiar with the track
  • 9:00am - Track goes green
  • 12:00 - 1:00pm - Lunch
  • 1:30 - 4:30pm - Track is green

Stay out as much or as little as you like when the track is green... 6 full hours of track time!

Price for the entire day including lunch is $595.00

Please contact GMG Racing for sign up at 714.432.1582, or feel free to email us at sales@gmgracing.com

Space is limited for maximum track time, so register early!
